The data describe cancer rates for all cancer sites by mortality, for all races, sexes, and ages, averaged over the years of 2009 - 2013. Rates are the number of deaths per 100,000 people.

Data was processed with QGIS 2.18 on Ubuntu 16 and the map is hosted on CARTO.
